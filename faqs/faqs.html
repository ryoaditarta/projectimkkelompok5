How to Rent a Car?

Renting a car with us is a breeze! First, pick the car that suits your needs. Then, you have the option to add any extra packages, like insurance and liability coverage, to your rental. Finally, proceed to the checkout, and you're all set for your journey!

What Kind of Car Can I See?

We've got you covered with an array of car options, from zippy Mini Cars to comfortable Sedans, practical Hatchbacks, and rugged SUVs. Choose the one that matches your style and travel needs.

Accepted Payments Method

We make payment a breeze with options to suit your preferences. You can pay via bank transfers, mobile banking transfers, or even through E-Money and retail stores. We aim to make the process as convenient as possible.

How does the Renting Contract Work

Once you've made your payment, we'll send you all the important details you need to know. You'll get info on where to find the car, the rental period, and instructions on how to return the car when you're done. It's simple and stress-free!

Refund Procedure

If your plans change, no problem. If you request a refund within 12 hours of creating the contract or before starting your trip, it's absolutely possible. We'll process the refund, returning the amount to the original payment method with minor service fees. We're here to ensure flexibility and satisfaction for our valued customers.
